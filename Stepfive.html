F-Follow Through.


The ball is released by a quick extension of the elbow and a flick of the wrist and fingers of the shooting hand. (Follow through is like reaching up and putting your hand in a cookie jar)


Ball should have back spin rotation which will enable the shooter to develop a softer touch.
